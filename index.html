About Project

Our Inspiration

 

Picture this: a frantic homeowner ankle-deep in a kitchen flood, desperately searching for a plumber. That was me. And that soggy debacle sparked an idea – NearMe . We're a team of problem-solvers on a mission to connect people with reliable services, born from personal experiences just like yours.

The Journey

 

Late-night brainstorming sessions, endless lines of code – every step brought us closer to our goal. As a Portfolio Project for Holberton School, NearMe represents our dedication to revolutionizing how people access essential services.
